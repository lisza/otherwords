Other Words

Other Words is an app to visualize translations.

Enter a word in the text box, and receive translations in 62 languages.

The words are displayed by similarity to the input word, and similarity between each other. Closely related words appear grouped together, and the groups furthest away from the input word are the least similar to it.

Hover over a word to see its language.

Note: Most language inputs should work, but results will be roman alphabet languages only.

Otherwords uses the Google Translate API. Word Similarity is determined with the help of the Levenshtein string distance algorithm in combination with first-letter grouping.
